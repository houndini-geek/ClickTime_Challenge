objective

Your objective is to click on the box as many times as you can within the time limit.

game controls

Click on the box to increase your score. Use the provided power-ups strategically to enhance your gameplay.

power_ups
time freeze:

stop the timer temporarily

time multiplier:

speeds up the timer for a limited duration

score boost:

doubles the points earned per click for a short period

gravity shift:

alter the mouvement of the box, making it more challenging to click

scoring

You earn one point for each click on the box. Every 50 points earned will grant you additional time.

game over

When the timer runs out, the game will display your final score. You can then choose to play again or exit.

hight score

Your highest score achieved in a single game will be recorded as your high score. Can you beat your own record?

tips and strategies

The rest button allows you to pause the game temporarily. When clicked, the game will pause, giving you a chance to take a break. You can resume the game by clicking the resume button. Keep in mind that you have a limited number of rest opportunities, so use them wisely.
